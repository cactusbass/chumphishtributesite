About CHUM

Chum is one of the most tenured Phish tribute acts in the country, having wowed auidences with incredible versions of Phish's most intricate compositions while routinely creating improvisionational magic in the spirit of the band they love. Known for their expansive library of Phish covers, they will tackle any challenge you can think up!

"Very few bands out there even dare to embrace the intricate discipline of executing these songs properly. San Francisco's Chum has earned elite status amongst the Phish community with their rare ability to capture the essence of the band's razor-sharp precision, organic improvisation and playful spirit. From 1.0 to 4.0, they have it all dialed. Even the most jaded vets nod in approval."

- Tyler Blue, veteran music promoter
